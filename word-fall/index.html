📝 How to Play Word Fall

1
Read falling letters — Watch as letters fall from the top of the screen. Identify letters you can use to form words.
2
Form words — Click or tap letters to select them and spell out valid words. Longer words earn more points.
3
Submit before time — Submit your word before the timer runs out. Speed matters — faster submissions earn bonus points.
4
Score points — Earn points for each valid word. Chain combos for multipliers and advance through increasingly challenging levels.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Word Fall?
Letters fall from the top of the screen. Click or tap the letters to form words, then submit before time runs out. Longer words score more points. Keep forming words to advance through levels.
Is Word Fall completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap letters to select them and form words.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Start with short 3-letter words to build momentum. Look for common prefixes like UN-, RE-, PRE- to form longer words quickly. Use frequently appearing letters first to clear space. Chain combos for multiplier bonuses and stay calm under time pressure.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
