🎵 How to Play Beat Rush

1
Listen to the beat — Pay attention to the rhythm and watch the notes scrolling toward the hit zone.
2
Tap on beat — Tap or click when each note reaches the hit zone. Perfect timing gives maximum points.
3
Chain combos — Hit consecutive notes without missing to build combo multipliers for higher scores.
4
Complete the song — Survive until the end of the track. Aim for a full combo to achieve the highest rank.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Beat Rush?
Listen to the rhythm and tap when notes reach the hit zone. Time your taps perfectly to score points. Chain consecutive hits to build combos and multiply your score. Complete the song to finish each level.
Is Beat Rush completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap the screen to hit the beats — works just like the desktop version.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Watch notes coming from ahead so you can prepare early. Tap in the center of the hit zone for perfect timing. The biggest scores come from combo streaks — consecutive hits multiply your points exponentially. Start with slower songs to learn the mechanics before tackling harder tracks.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
