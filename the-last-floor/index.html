❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play The Last Floor?
Navigate through dungeon floors by clicking or tapping to move and interact. Fight enemies in combat, collect items and power-ups, and manage your resources to reach the final floor. Each run is different thanks to procedural generation.
Is The Last Floor completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Use tap controls to navigate, fight, and manage your inventory.
What's the best strategy for reaching the last floor?
Don't rush — explore each floor fully for items before descending. Manage your health carefully and save healing items for boss fights. Upgrade attack first, then defense. Learn enemy patterns and prioritize dangerous enemies in multi-enemy encounters.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
