❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are all these tools really free?

Yes — 27+ tools, completely free. No signup, no ads, no usage limits. Every tool runs 100% client-side in your browser. The source code is on GitHub.

Do you collect my data?

No. Every tool processes your data entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Check the Network tab in DevTools — zero outgoing requests with your data.

Can I use these tools offline?

Most tools work offline once loaded since they are pure client-side HTML/CSS/JS. Bookmark the page and use it without internet.

What is DonFlow?

DonFlow is a local-first budget tracker that compares your planned budget against actual spending. It detects "budget drift" — where reality diverges from your plan. Your financial data never leaves your browser. Try the demo →

How do I share a tool result with my team?

Many tools have a 🔗 Share button that encodes your input into a URL. Send the link to anyone — they'll see exactly what you see, no signup required.
