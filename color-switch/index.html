🎨 How to Play Color Switch

1
Tap to Jump — Tap or click anywhere on the screen to make your ball bounce upward through the obstacles.
2
Watch Your Color — Your ball has a specific color. Only pass through barrier segments that match your current color.
3
Time the Barriers — Barriers rotate continuously. Wait for the matching color segment to align before jumping through.
4
Collect Stars — Grab stars between barriers to increase your score. Stars change your ball's color — adapt quickly!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Color Switch?
Tap or click to make your ball jump upward. Your ball has a color — only pass through barrier segments that match it. Mistiming or hitting the wrong color ends the game. Collect stars between barriers to score points.
Is Color Switch completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap anywhere on the screen to jump — actually plays better on mobile since it's a tap-based game.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Be patient at each barrier — wait for the matching color segment to rotate into position. Use short, controlled taps for precise height control. Stay near the center of the screen for maximum reaction time. After each star pickup, immediately identify your new color before approaching the next barrier.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access anytime.
