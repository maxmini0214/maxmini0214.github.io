🔀 How to Play Merge Mania

1
Select — Tap or click an item on the board to pick it up.
2
Merge — Drag it onto a matching item to combine them into a higher-level piece.
3
Chain — Keep merging to discover new items and build combo chains for bonus points.
4
Manage Space — Keep free spaces on the board. If the grid fills up completely, it's game over!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Merge Mania?
Drag or tap matching items to merge them into higher-level items. Keep merging to discover new items and reach the highest tier. Plan your board space carefully — if the grid fills up, the game ends.
Is Merge Mania completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Use tap-and-drag touch controls to merge items.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Keep your highest-level items in one corner and build merge chains toward them. Don't spread merges across the board — focus on one area. Always keep free spaces available and plan 2-3 merges ahead for cascade combos.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
