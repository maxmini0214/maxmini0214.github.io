💣 How to Play Minesweeper

1
Click to reveal — Left-click (or tap) any tile to uncover it. Your first click is always safe.
2
Read the numbers — Each number tells you how many mines are in the 8 surrounding tiles. Use logic to deduce safe tiles.
3
Flag mines — Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag tiles you think are mines. This prevents accidental clicks.
4
Clear the field — Reveal every non-mine tile to win. One wrong click on a mine = game over. Think before you click!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Minesweeper?
Left-click to reveal a tile. Numbers show how many of the 8 surrounding tiles contain mines. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag suspected mines. Reveal all safe tiles to win!
Is Minesweeper completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap to reveal, long-press to flag.
What do the numbers mean?
Each number tells you exactly how many mines are in the 8 tiles surrounding it. A "1" means one adjacent mine, "2" means two, etc. A blank tile means zero adjacent mines — and automatically reveals its neighbors.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
