🍎 How to Play Fruit Catch

1
Move Your Basket — Use arrow keys, A/D keys, or drag on mobile to slide your basket left and right.
2
Catch Good Fruits — Position your basket under falling fruits. Each fruit adds to your score — some are worth more than others.
3
Avoid Bad Items — Watch out for rotten fruits or bombs. Catching them costs you a life!
4
Survive the Speed Up — The game gets faster as your score climbs. Stay centered and keep your reflexes sharp.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Fruit Catch?
Move your basket left and right with arrow keys, A/D, or by dragging on mobile. Catch falling fruits to score points. Avoid rotten fruits and bombs — they cost lives. The game speeds up as your score increases!
Is Fruit Catch completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Drag your finger to move the basket — touch controls are smooth and precise.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Keep your basket near the center so you can reach fruits on either side. Focus on catching high-value special fruits when they appear. Don't risk a life chasing a low-value fruit near a rotten one. Watch the top of the screen to anticipate where the next fruits will drop.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access anytime.
