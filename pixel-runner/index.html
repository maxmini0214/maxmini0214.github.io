🏃 How to Play Pixel Runner

1
Run — Your character runs automatically. Focus on timing your actions.
2
Jump — Tap the screen or press Space / Up arrow to jump over obstacles.
3
Collect — Grab power-ups like shields and double jumps to survive longer.
4
Survive — Speed increases over time. Beat your high score by surviving as long as possible!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Pixel Runner?
Tap or press Space/Up to jump over obstacles. Time your jumps carefully as the speed increases. Collect power-ups for shields and double jumps. The longer you survive, the higher your score.
Is Pixel Runner completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap anywhere on screen to jump — simple one-touch controls.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Focus on timing — jump at the last possible moment for maximum control. Learn obstacle patterns as they repeat. Prioritize shield power-ups over score bonuses. Stay calm at high speeds and watch the far right side of the screen to react early.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
