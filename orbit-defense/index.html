🛸 How to Play Orbit Defense

1
Place Towers — Click or tap to position defense towers at strategic points around your orbital station.
2
Defend Waves — Enemy ships arrive in waves. Your towers automatically fire at enemies within range.
3
Upgrade — Earn resources from defeated enemies. Upgrade towers for more damage, range, and special effects.
4
Survive — Each wave gets harder with new enemy types and bosses. Adapt your strategy to survive!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Orbit Defense?
Place defense towers around your orbital station to intercept incoming enemy waves. Click or tap to position towers, upgrade them between waves, and use special abilities to survive. Each wave gets harder with new enemy types.
Is Orbit Defense completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap to place and upgrade towers with intuitive touch controls.
What's the best strategy for surviving late waves?
Mix tower types for maximum coverage — use slow towers to reduce enemy speed, then place high-damage towers along the path. Upgrade existing towers before buying new ones. Save special abilities for boss waves and always cover multiple lanes.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
