❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Math Dash?
A math equation appears on screen. Calculate the answer in your head and select the correct option from the choices before the timer runs out. Each correct answer earns points and keeps the clock going.
Is Math Dash completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap your answer to submit.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Start with easy operations to build speed. Memorize multiplication tables for faster recall. Estimate before calculating to eliminate wrong answers quickly. Use number patterns and stay focused on maintaining speed without sacrificing accuracy.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
