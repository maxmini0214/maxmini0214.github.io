❓ FAQ

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs 100% in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.

What formats are supported?

You can drop JPEG, PNG, and WebP images. Output is JPEG or WebP — both offer excellent compression.

How much can I reduce file size?

Typical reductions are 50–80% for photos at quality 70–80%, with minimal visible quality loss.

Is there a file size limit?

No hard limit — it depends on your browser's memory. Most browsers handle images up to 50MB.

Can I compress multiple images?

One at a time currently. Drop a new image anytime to compress another.
