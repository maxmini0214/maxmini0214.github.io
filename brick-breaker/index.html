🧱 How to Play Brick Breaker

1
Launch — Click or tap to launch the ball. It bounces off walls, the ceiling, and your paddle.
2
Move the paddle — Use mouse, arrow keys, or drag your finger to keep the ball in play.
3
Break bricks — The ball destroys bricks on contact. Some bricks need multiple hits. Catch power-ups for boosts.
4
Clear all levels — Don't let the ball fall! Clear every brick to advance. Aim for the high score.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Brick Breaker?
Move the paddle left and right to bounce the ball. The ball breaks bricks on contact. Clear all bricks to advance. Catch falling power-ups for special abilities like multi-ball or wider paddle!
Is Brick Breaker completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Drag your finger to move the paddle — smooth and intuitive.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Aim for the top rows first — clearing upper bricks creates chain reactions as the ball bounces around above. Keep your paddle centered for maximum reaction time. Hit the ball with the paddle edge to control angle. Prioritize multi-ball power-ups over everything else.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
