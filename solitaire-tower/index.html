🃏 How to Play Solitaire Tower

1
Check the draw pile — Look at the face-up card on your draw pile. This is your active card.
2
Match ±1 — Click any uncovered tower card that is exactly one higher or lower than the active card. Kings wrap to Aces.
3
Build chains — Keep removing cards in a row without drawing for combo bonuses. Longer chains = higher scores.
4
Clear the tower — Remove all cards from the tower to win. If stuck, draw from the stock pile to get a new active card.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Solitaire Tower?
Click cards that are one higher or one lower than the current draw pile card. Chain combos by removing multiple cards in a row. Clear all cards from the tower to win. Kings wrap to Aces and vice versa.
Is Solitaire Tower completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap cards to play — works just like the desktop version.
What's the best strategy for high scores?
Focus on uncovering face-down cards first — they reveal more options. Build long chains for combo bonuses. Save your stock pile draws for when you truly have no matches. Remember: Kings wrap to Aces, so you can chain K→A→2 or 2→A→K.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
