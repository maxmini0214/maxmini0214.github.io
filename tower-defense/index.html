🏰 How to Play Tower Defense

1
Scout the map — Identify choke points and narrow paths where enemies must pass through.
2
Place towers — Click or tap valid spots to build towers. Mix types for coverage: damage, slow, splash.
3
Defend & earn — Defeat enemies to earn gold. Upgrade existing towers between waves for stronger defenses.
4
Adapt — Enemy types change each wave. Fast enemies, armored tanks, flying units — match your towers to the threat.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Tower Defense?
Place towers along the enemy path by clicking or tapping on valid spots. Each tower type has different range, damage, and abilities. Earn gold from defeating enemies to buy and upgrade towers. Survive all waves to win!
Is Tower Defense completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No download, no signup, no ads, no in-app purchases. Play instantly in any modern browser.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and all modern mobile browsers. Tap to place towers and manage upgrades.
What's the best tower placement strategy?
Focus on choke points — narrow paths where enemies must pass. Pair slow towers with high-damage towers for devastating combos. Upgrade existing towers before building new ones; a maxed-out tower usually outperforms two basic ones.
Is my data collected while playing?
No. The game runs 100% in your browser with zero server communication. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your gameplay stays completely private.
Can I play offline?
After the page loads once, the game runs entirely in your browser. If your connection drops mid-game, you can keep playing. Bookmark the page for quick access.
